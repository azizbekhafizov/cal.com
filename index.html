01. Connect all your calendars

Cal.com checks for conflicts across all of your calendars and only offers times that are open. Never get double booked again.

02. Workflow automation

Cal.com enables you to build processes around your events. Notifications, reminders and follow-ups are automatically taken care of.

03. Scheduling for your team

Round-Robin scheduling ensures even distribution of calls across your team. Collective availability makes it easy to book your team when everyone is available.

04. Route bookers to the right person

Ensure every booker is connected to the right person using Routing Forms. Automatically connect bookers to the right person, event or even to a link.

05. Avoid meeting overload

Limit people from overbooking you on a weekly or daily basis. Put breathing room between meetings with buffers and prevent surprises with minimum notice periods.
